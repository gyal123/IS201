  1. Colonial Town
    • Located in the heart of Quito, the colonial town is known for its stunning architecture, historic churches, charming museums, and cozy coffee shops.
    • The top three must-visit spots in Quito’s Colonial Town are San Francisco Church, the Archbishop’s Palace, and La Compañía de Jesús Church.

  2. Mitad del Mundo
    • After paying the entrance fee, you can explore small museums, take part in recreational activities, and discover more about Ecuadorian culture!
    • I recommend going on the weekends, as traditional dance performances are held in one of the main plazas.
  3. El Panecillo
    • It’s a large statue of the Virgin Mary, and visitors can reach the top to enjoy stunning views of the city.
    • The sunset from the top is absolutely breathtaking — it's a perfect spot for capturing stunning photos!
